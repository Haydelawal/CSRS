About CSRS

Campus Security Report System (CSRS)

Campus Security is traditionally defined as one of the most pervasive problems that a campus or university must address. There rarely has a campus issue, problem, or challenge that requires the recruitment of everyone in the university to solve. Security is an essential issue for every organization i.e. government, private, commercial or educational institution. Thus, university security personnel such as campus security officers and campus security guard need to have a strong understanding of campus security.
